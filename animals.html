Animals in Yellowstone

There are 67 different species of animals in Yellowstone

Gray wolves were reintroduced into Yellowstone in 1995

The largest bison herd of 5,000 can be found in Yellowstone

Nearly 300 bird species have been found in Yellowstone

Reptiles aren't studied that much in Yellowstone, but the bullsnake is the largest of 6 species found
