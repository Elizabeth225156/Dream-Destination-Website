Locations in Yellowstone

Caldera

Yellowstone Caldera, also known as the Yellowstone Plateau Volcanic Field, is a Quaternary caldera complex and volcanic plateau spanning parts of Wyoming, Idaho, and Montana. It is driven by the Yellowstone hotspot and is largely within Yellowstone National Park (Wikipedia)

Head to the overlook next to the Washburn Hot Springs to see the Caldera
